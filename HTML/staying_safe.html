Protecting yourself from COVID-19
Follow These Steps To Lower Your Chances of Contracting COVID-19
  • Wash your hands regularly, with soap and water, or an alcohol-based hand sanitizer
  • Wear a mask in public places, especially indoors
  • Practice social distancing to reduce your chance of coming into contact with the virus
  • Keep up to date with vaccinations and boosters
